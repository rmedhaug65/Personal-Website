Examining The Telecom Landscape - Part 1

This is what I hope to be the first part in a series on the history of how the internet came to be, starting with the telecommunication landscape. My goal in this first piece is to lay the ground work with a brief history of the telecommunication industry, focussing primarily on the evolution of the telephone and cable companies.
